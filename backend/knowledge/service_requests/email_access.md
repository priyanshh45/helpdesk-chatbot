# Email Access Request

## Purpose

Provide authorized users with access to their corporate email account.

---

## Scope

Applicable to:

- New employees
- Existing employees requiring mailbox access
- Users changing departments

---

## Prerequisites

- Manager approval
- Valid employee account
- Microsoft 365 license available

---

## Procedure

### Step 1

Receive the service request.

---

### Step 2

Verify user identity.

---

### Step 3

Verify approvals.

---

### Step 4

Assign Microsoft 365 license.

---

### Step 5

Create or enable mailbox.

---

### Step 6

Configure Outlook.

---

### Step 7

Test email sending and receiving.

---

### Step 8

Share login instructions with the user.

---

## Common Issues

- License unavailable
- Mailbox synchronization delay
- Incorrect email configuration
- Authentication issues

---

## Escalation

Escalate if:

- Mailbox cannot be created.
- Exchange service is unavailable.
- Licensing issues exist.
