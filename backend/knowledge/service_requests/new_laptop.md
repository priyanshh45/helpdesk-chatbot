# New Laptop Request

## Purpose

This document describes the standard process for requesting and issuing a new company laptop.

---

## Scope

Applicable to:

- New employees
- Existing employees requiring replacement
- Employees with approved hardware upgrades

---

## Prerequisites

Before processing the request:

- Manager approval obtained.
- Laptop available in inventory.
- User account created (if applicable).
- Asset record ready.

---

## Procedure

### Step 1

Receive the service request.

---

### Step 2

Verify management approval.

---

### Step 3

Check laptop availability in inventory.

---

### Step 4

Assign the laptop asset to the employee.

---

### Step 5

Install the standard operating system.

---

### Step 6

Install approved software.

Examples:

- Microsoft Office
- VPN Client
- Antivirus
- Microsoft Teams

---

### Step 7

Configure user account.

---

### Step 8

Verify internet connectivity.

---

### Step 9

Hand over the laptop.

---

### Step 10

Update the Asset Management System.

---

## Common Issues

- Device unavailable
- Missing approvals
- Incorrect asset allocation
- Software installation failure

---

## Escalation

Escalate if:

- Inventory is unavailable.
- Procurement is required.
- Hardware is defective.
