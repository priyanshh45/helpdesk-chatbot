# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is an Incident?

An incident is an unexpected interruption or reduction in the quality of an IT service that requires restoration as quickly as possible.

---

## What is a Service Request?

A Service Request is a request for a standard IT service such as software installation, VPN access, or a new laptop.

---

## What is the difference between an Incident and a Service Request?

| Incident | Service Request |
|----------|-----------------|
| Unexpected issue | Planned request |
| Service interruption | Standard service |
| Restore service | Fulfill request |

---

## What is ITIL?

ITIL is a framework of best practices for managing IT services and improving service quality.

---

## What is ITSM?

IT Service Management (ITSM) is the practice of designing, delivering, supporting, and improving IT services.

---

## What is an SLA?

A Service Level Agreement (SLA) defines the expected response and resolution times for IT services.

---

## When should I contact the Service Desk?

Contact the Service Desk when:

- You cannot access company systems.
- You need software installed.
- Your password has expired.
- Your laptop or desktop is not functioning correctly.
- You need access to company resources.

---

## How do I report an incident?

Provide:

- Your name
- Device name
- Department
- Description of the issue
- Error message
- Screenshot (if available)

---

## How do I request a new laptop?

Submit a Service Request with manager approval. The IT team will verify inventory, configure the device, and assign it to you.
