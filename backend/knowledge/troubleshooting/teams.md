# Microsoft Teams Troubleshooting

## Purpose

Resolve common Microsoft Teams issues.

---

## Common Symptoms

- Teams not opening
- Login failure
- Audio not working
- Camera not working
- Poor call quality

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Restart Microsoft Teams.
2. Sign out and sign in again.
3. Check internet connectivity.
4. Clear Teams cache.
5. Update Teams.
6. Verify microphone and camera permissions.
7. Restart the computer.

---

## Escalation

Escalate if:

- Microsoft Teams service is unavailable.
- Multiple users experience the same issue.
- Licensing issue exists.
