# ITSM Glossary

## ITSM

IT Service Management

---

## ITIL

Information Technology Infrastructure Library

---

## SLA

Service Level Agreement

---

## Incident

An unplanned interruption to an IT service.

---

## Service Request

A formal request for a standard IT service.

---

## Problem

The root cause of one or more incidents.

---

## Change

A planned modification to an IT service or infrastructure.

---

## Service Desk

The single point of contact between users and IT support.

---

## Asset

Any hardware or software owned by the organization.

---

## CMDB

Configuration Management Database used to store information about IT assets and their relationships.

---

## CAB

Change Advisory Board responsible for reviewing and approving significant changes.

---

## VPN

Virtual Private Network used to securely access company resources remotely.

---

## Active Directory

Microsoft directory service used for authentication and user management.

---

## Microsoft 365

Cloud productivity platform including Outlook, Teams, OneDrive, and Office applications.
