# Printer Issue SOP

## Purpose

Provide a structured troubleshooting process for resolving printer-related incidents.

---

## Common Symptoms

- Printer offline
- Paper jam
- Print queue stuck
- Unable to print
- Slow printing
- Poor print quality
- Printer not detected
- Driver error

---

## Initial Checks

Before troubleshooting:

- Verify printer power.
- Check cables.
- Ensure the printer is connected to the network.
- Confirm the correct printer is selected.

---

## Troubleshooting Procedure

### Step 1

Restart the printer.

---

### Step 2

Restart the user's computer.

---

### Step 3

Verify the printer status.

If the printer shows **Offline**, reconnect it to the network.

---

### Step 4

Clear the print queue.

Delete any stuck print jobs.

---

### Step 5

Restart the Print Spooler service.

---

### Step 6

Update or reinstall the printer driver if necessary.

---

### Step 7

Print a test page.

---

### Step 8

Confirm the issue is resolved with the user.

---

## Common Causes

- Network connectivity issue
- Incorrect printer selection
- Printer offline
- Driver corruption
- Print Spooler stopped
- Paper jam
- Low toner
- Hardware fault

---

## Escalation

Escalate if:

- Printer hardware is damaged.
- Network connectivity cannot be restored.
- Driver installation repeatedly fails.
- Multiple users report the same issue.

---

## Best Practices

- Keep printer drivers updated.
- Use approved drivers.
- Remove stuck print jobs promptly.
- Perform regular printer maintenance.
